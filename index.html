Make and Recieve Payments Easily

People can register on our service, and instead of having a phone, they can simply use these keys to make and receive payments. With NFC support, these keys can be used with credit card terminals, ATMs and mobile phones for making transactions seamlessly with a single touch. This eliminates the need of mobile phones and are way more portable than credit cards with increased accessibility and the ability to connect with a wide range of devices.

Biometric Security

With biometric support, PlutoKeys uses a person's natural identity than their documents, thus aiding the undocumented. Payment security is enhanced with the help of biometric authentication.

KYC - Know Your Customer

The KYC (or know your customer) system verifies the legitimacy and ensures security of our customers.

Pluto Agent

At government registered and verified shops, we will establish kiosks with our software and have shopkeepers work as Pluto Agents to help customers make transcations, while earning money through a referral system.
